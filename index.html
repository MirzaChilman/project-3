Information

PRICE

All shown and free to everyone

WHAT TO BRING

Since this is an open space, organizer does not provide you with any kind of convenient support, you are more than welcome to bring snack, camping chair, blanket pillows. Other thing you should consider to bring:

  1. Girlfriend/Boyfriend (if you have one of course)
  2. Family (Best to enjoy the movie with your family)
  3. Credit Card/Cash (there will be lots of snack and merchandise that you can buy)
  4. Beer!

NB : please bring your own medicine in case something happen, the vendor will also provide medical support when needed
